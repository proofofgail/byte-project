About

The Byte Project is a curatorial platform at the intersection of art and technology. We commission and develop new work by artists who harness advanced technologies, building a programme of exhibitions, residencies, and public events that expand both the practice and the audience.

The platform

The Byte Project began as a dinner series designed to bring together innovative minds from the arts, technology, and investment sectors. Over shared meals, drinks, and conversation, the series explored consumption, community, and connection — one byte at a time.

Since then, The Byte Project has evolved into a curatorial platform dedicated to exploring the intersections of interdisciplinary artistic practices within art and technology. The platform supports artists who harness advanced technologies while expanding the reach and engagement of their audiences through a variety of projects.

We curate and develop spaces where radical thought and artistic projects thrive — across physical and digital formats, in London and beyond.

Approach

Our work begins with the artists — listening, researching, and building the conditions for genuinely new work to emerge at the frontier of art and technology.

Curatorial experimentation

The Byte Project operates as a laboratory. We take risks, test ideas, and work with artists in development — before the work is finished, before the category exists. The programme changes because the field changes.

Institutional reach

We build relationships with museums, foundations, and universities to bring the work into institutional contexts — securing support, expanding audiences, and situating digital and technology-based art within the broader cultural conversation.

Community and access

We believe art should be an experience, and everyone should benefit. The platform's events and programmes are designed to bring new audiences into contact with ambitious work — not just collectors and institutions, but the wider public.

Long form thinking

Through Byte Press, our independent publishing arm, we give commissioned work a permanent form. Publications, editions, and collected writings that outlast any individual exhibition or event and contribute to the field's ongoing discourse.

Byte Press

Byte Press is our independent publishing arm.

A publishing house producing a wide range of high-quality special editions and collector-focused works at the intersection of art, technology, and culture. Byte Press transforms books into lasting cultural objects that educate, inspire, and endure — beyond any single exhibition, programme, or moment.

While rooted in The Byte Project's commissions, Byte Press operates independently — publishing artists, writers, curators, and researchers across the full territory of art and technology.
